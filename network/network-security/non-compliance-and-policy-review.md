# Non-Compliance and Policy Review

**This page defines the consequences of policy violations and the review cycle for the network security policy.**

### Non-compliance

Violations of this policy may result in:

* Device disconnection from the company network
* Disciplinary action
* Termination of employment for serious violations

### Policy review

This policy must be reviewed annually, or earlier if new threats or organizational changes require updates.
