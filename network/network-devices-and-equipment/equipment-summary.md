### Equipment role summary

#### Router

Routers provide WAN connectivity and support traffic between sites and external networks.

#### Switch

Switches provide campus switching, local device connectivity, and PoE support where needed.

#### Firewall

Firewalls protect the network perimeter and inspect inbound and outbound traffic.

#### Access Point

Access points provide wireless connectivity for employee and guest networks.

#### Wire

Copper and fiber cabling provide structured links for endpoint access and high-speed backbone connections.
